Bible Study

Getting Started with Scripture

The Bible is the foundation of Christian faith and practice. Whether you are new to the faith or a long-time believer, regular engagement with Scripture is essential for spiritual growth.

  • For beginners: Start with the Gospel of John to understand who Jesus is.
  • For prayer: The Psalms are a treasury of honest, heartfelt prayer.
  • For daily life: Proverbs offers practical wisdom for every situation.
  • For doctrine: Paul's letters (Romans, Ephesians, Galatians) explain the depth of salvation.

Devotional Practices

Daily Quiet Time

Setting aside even 15–20 minutes each morning for Bible reading and prayer transforms your day. Choose a consistent time and place, read a passage, reflect on what God is saying, and respond in prayer.

Journaling Your Faith

Keeping a spiritual journal helps you track God's faithfulness over time. Record answers to prayer, insights from Scripture, and moments where you sense God's presence. Reviewing past entries deepens gratitude and trust.
