⭐ Saints & Inspirations

Holy men and women who prepared for Christ and now intercede for us

"Therefore, since we are surrounded by such a great cloud of witnesses, let us throw off everything that hinders and the sin that so easily entangles."

— Hebrews 12:1

Our Patrons & Intercessors

🌹

Our Lady of Fátima

Feast Day: May 13

The Blessed Virgin Mary appeared at Fátima in 1917, urging prayer, penance, and the Rosary. Her message — "Pray, pray very much!" — echoes the call to prepare for Christ's return.

📿

St. Pio of Pietrelcina

Feast Day: September 23

Padre Pio bore the stigmata and dedicated his life to prayer, confession, and the Mass. He said: "Pray, hope, and don't worry." His love for the Eucharist inspires our Holy Hour.

❤️‍🔥

St. Margaret Mary Alacoque

Feast Day: October 16

To St. Margaret Mary, Jesus revealed His Sacred Heart and asked for a Holy Hour every Thursday night. Her "yes" gave the world the Devotion to the Sacred Heart.

✝️

St. John Paul II

Feast Day: October 22

Pope John Paul II was a powerful witness of faith. He prayed the Rosary daily and encouraged all Catholics: "Do not be afraid!" His life was a constant Holy Hour before God.

🕊️

St. Teresa of Calcutta

Feast Day: September 5

Mother Teresa saw Jesus in every person she served. She began each day with Mass and a Holy Hour, crediting prayer as the source of her strength to love the poorest of the poor.

📖

St. Faustina Kowalska

Feast Day: October 5

Apostle of Divine Mercy. Jesus appeared to her and said: "Mankind will not have peace until it turns with trust to My mercy." She reminds us to run to Jesus with confidence.
