What to Expect

A Typical Holy Hour

Each gathering is a dedicated time of prayer, Scripture reading, and spiritual fellowship. Sessions typically include:

  • Opening praise and worship
  • Scripture reading and meditation
  • Group and personal intercessory prayer
  • Time of silent contemplation
  • Closing prayer and blessing

Special Gatherings

Seasonal Prayer Services

Throughout the year we hold extended prayer services during the Christian seasons of Advent, Lent, and Pentecost, as well as special community prayer nights. Announcements will be made at regular gatherings and posted here when scheduled.

✝ All gatherings are open to Christians of all denominations. Whether you are new to prayer or have walked with Christ for many years, you are welcome here.
