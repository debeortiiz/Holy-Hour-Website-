Special Observances

🌟

First Friday

A special Holy Hour in reparation to the Sacred Heart of Jesus. All-night adoration for those who can attend.

🌹

First Saturday

Devotion to the Immaculate Heart of Mary. Confession, Rosary, Mass, and 15-minute meditation on the mysteries.

🕯️

Advent & Lent

Extended Holy Hours during Advent and Lent to prepare our hearts for Christmas and Easter — and for Christ's return.

Can't Attend in Person?

You can join us spiritually! Set aside one hour each day to pray the Rosary, read Scripture, or simply sit quietly in God's presence. You can also contact us to receive our prayer intentions by email. No matter where you are, you can keep a Holy Hour with Jesus.
