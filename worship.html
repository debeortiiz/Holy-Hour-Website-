Why We Worship

"God is spirit, and his worshipers must worship in the Spirit and in truth." — John 4:24

Worship is more than music — it is the posture of the heart turned toward God in adoration, gratitude, and surrender. Every Holy Hour gathering includes time for praise because worship prepares our hearts to hear and receive from God.

Psalm 150

Praise the Lord!
Praise God in his sanctuary;
praise him in his mighty heavens.
Praise him for his acts of power;
praise him for his surpassing greatness.
Let everything that has breath praise the Lord.
Praise the Lord!

Worship in Daily Life

Beyond the Gathering

Worship is not confined to Sunday services. Romans 12:1 calls us to offer our whole lives as "a living sacrifice, holy and pleasing to God — this is your true and proper worship." Singing, serving, giving, and living faithfully are all acts of worship.
