Frequently Asked Questions

Is Holy Hour free to use?

Yes! Holy Hour is completely free to download and use. We believe that the Bible and anything that has to do with God should be free. The Gospel is for everyone, and there should be no exceptions.

Is Holy Hour for my denomination?

Absolutely. Holy Hour is designed for all Christian denominations. Whether you are Baptist, Pentecostal, Methodist, non-denominational, or any other tradition, you will find Holy Hour valuable. We focus on what unites us: Jesus Christ.

Can I submit prayer requests through the app?

Yes! You can submit prayer requests through the app or through our contact page. Deborah personally prays over every request submitted.

How do I report a problem with the app?

If you experience any issues, please contact us at cristovieneprontobydeborah@gmail.com. We will respond as quickly as possible.

What languages is the app available in?

The Holy Hour app is currently available in English, Spanish, French, Portuguese, and German. We are actively working to add more languages in the future so that everyone can encounter God in their native tongue. Cristo Viene Pronto!
