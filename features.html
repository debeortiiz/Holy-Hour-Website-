App Features

Everything you need to grow in faith, build a prayer life, and stay connected to God's Word — all in one place.

What Holy Hour Offers

Designed for believers of all backgrounds, Holy Hour gives you the tools to make every day a day of faith.

🙏

Daily Prayer & Devotion

Start every day with purpose. Holy Hour guides you through structured prayer times, morning devotionals, and evening reflections designed to help you build a consistent, powerful prayer life.

  • Morning and evening prayer prompts
  • Guided intercessory prayer
  • Personal prayer journaling
  • Prayer reminders and streaks
📖

Scripture Reading

Dive deeper into God's Word with daily Scripture readings, verse highlights, and thematic Bible study plans. God's Word is living and active — let it speak to you every day.

  • Daily verse of the day
  • Reading plans and devotional series
  • Verse highlighting and notes
  • Search and bookmarking
🌱

Spiritual Growth Tools

Track your spiritual journey and celebrate your growth. Holy Hour helps you set faith goals, monitor your consistency, and reflect on how far God has brought you.

  • Faith goal setting
  • Spiritual journey timeline
  • Gratitude and reflection prompts
  • Progress tracking and milestones
⛪

Find a Church

No one should walk this faith journey alone. Holy Hour helps you find a local church where you can worship, grow, and connect with other believers in your community.

  • Church directory by location
  • Service times and contact info
  • Multiple denominations listed
  • Easy-to-use map integration
✉️

Prayer Requests

Submit your prayer needs directly to Deborah and the Holy Hour prayer team. We believe in the power of intercessory prayer, and we are honored to stand with you before the Lord.

  • Submit prayer requests anytime
  • Personal prayer by Deborah
  • Confidential and compassionate
  • Direct email response available

✝ All Denominations Welcome

Holy Hour is for every believer in Jesus Christ. Whether you are Baptist, Pentecostal, Methodist, Presbyterian, non-denominational, or any other tradition — you are welcome here. We are united by our love for Christ.
