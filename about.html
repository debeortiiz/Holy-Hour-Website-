About Holy Hour

A mission born from faith — helping believers deepen their relationship with God through prayer, scripture, and community.

Our Mission

Bringing You Closer to God

Holy Hour was created with a simple but profound purpose: to give every believer a beautiful, accessible space to spend meaningful time with God every single day.

We believe that a daily holy hour — one dedicated hour of prayer, scripture, and reflection — can transform lives, families, and communities.

Our app brings together everything you need for your spiritual journey: guided prayers, daily scripture readings, the rosary, liturgical calendars, and a supportive community of believers around the world.
