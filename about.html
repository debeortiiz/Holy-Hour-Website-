Our Mission

Cristo Viene Pronto by Deborah is a Holy Hour ministry dedicated to reminding believers that Christ is coming soon. Through prayer, adoration, and scripture, we invite all souls to prepare their hearts and draw closer to Jesus.

What Is a Holy Hour?

A Holy Hour is a dedicated time — traditionally one hour — spent in prayer before the Lord. This ancient practice of the Church invites us to sit in His presence, offer thanksgiving, seek forgiveness, and intercede for the world. Jesus asked His disciples: "Could you not watch with me one hour?" (Matthew 26:40). Our ministry answers that call.

About Deborah

Deborah founded this ministry out of a deep love for Jesus and a desire to share that love with others. Inspired by the urgency of the Gospel message — Cristo Viene Pronto (Christ Is Coming Soon) — she created this website as a spiritual resource for all who seek to prepare their hearts and grow in faith.

What We Offer

This website includes daily prayers, a guide to praying the Rosary, inspirational stories of the saints, Holy Hour schedules, and a way to connect with our community. All are welcome — regardless of where you are on your spiritual journey.
