About Holy Hour

Our mission is to help every believer draw closer to God through prayer, Scripture, and spiritual community.

✝ Our Mission

Holy Hour was created to give every Christian a simple, beautiful space to spend dedicated time with God. Whether you have five minutes or a full hour, our app guides you through prayer, Scripture reading, and spiritual reflection.

We believe that consistent time in God's presence transforms hearts, families, and communities. Cristo Viene Pronto — Christ is coming soon — and we want to help as many people as possible be ready to meet Him.

🌍 Our Vision

We envision a global Christian community — united across all denominations — that prioritizes prayer and the Word of God. Holy Hour is a tool for every believer, regardless of their background, tradition, or church affiliation.

We are not denominational. We welcome Baptists, Pentecostals, Methodists, non-denominational believers, and everyone in between. If you love Jesus, you are welcome here.

🙏 Deborah's Prayer Ministry

Holy Hour was founded by Deborah, a passionate servant of Christ whose heart is to intercede for others. Through this platform, Deborah invites you to submit your prayer requests so that she can personally pray over your needs.

No request is too big or too small. You are loved, you are seen, and you are prayed for. Reach out anytime at cristovieneprontobydeborah@gmail.com.
