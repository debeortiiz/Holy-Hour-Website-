Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026 · We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Holy Hour ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with the terms, please discontinue use of our app and website.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information (name, email address) when you register
  • Prayer intentions and journal entries you create
  • Messages sent through our contact form
  • Preferences and settings you configure

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Prayer streaks and activity (stored locally on your device by default)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our app and services
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our app
  • Send you daily scripture notifications (if enabled)
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve the experience
  • Protect against fraud and abuse

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you choose to share prayer intentions with the community
  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party services for analytics and hosting (bound by confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Data Storage and Security

Your prayer journal entries and personal reflections are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption to protect your data.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we strive to use the best commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from notifications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@holyhour.app.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages; however, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality only (session management). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; however, this may affect some website functionality.

9. Third-Party Links

Our app and website may contain links to third-party websites (such as the App Store or Google Play). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@holyhour.app
  • Website: Contact Form

Your privacy matters to us. Holy Hour is built on trust — we handle your personal information with the same care and respect we bring to every aspect of our ministry. May God bless you. 🙏
