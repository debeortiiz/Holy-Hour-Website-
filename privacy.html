🔒 ZERO DATA COLLECTION POLICY

Holy Hour does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Holy Hour ("we," "us," or "our") is fully committed to your privacy. We have designed our app and website so that your personal information is never collected, stored, or transmitted — by us or anyone else through our Service.

2. We Do NOT Collect Any Data

Holy Hour does not collect:

  • ❌ Personal names or email addresses
  • ❌ Prayer requests or personal messages
  • ❌ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ❌ Analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics
  • ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
  • ❌ Cookies, trackers, or fingerprinting data
  • ❌ Account or authentication information

3. Local Storage Only

Any preferences or settings you choose within the app are stored exclusively on your own device. We never receive, access, or store any of this information on our servers. Your spiritual journey remains entirely private.

4. No Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party data services, including:

  • ❌ Google Analytics or Firebase
  • ❌ Facebook SDK or tracking pixels
  • ❌ Crash reporting or monitoring services
  • ❌ Advertising networks of any kind

5. No Ads, No Tracking

Holy Hour contains no advertisements and does not track your behavior, preferences, or usage patterns in any way.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliant)

Holy Hour is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Because we collect zero data, there are no privacy risks for children or families using our Service.

7. Your Data Is Always Safe

✅ Your information stays on your device only
✅ We never access or store anything on our servers
✅ No data to be hacked, sold, or shared
✅ 100% private and secure

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. However, our commitment to zero data collection will never change. We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page to reflect any revisions.

9. Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • ✓ COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • ✓ GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • ✓ CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
