The Call to Fellowship

"And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, not giving up meeting together, as some are in the habit of doing, but encouraging one another." — Hebrews 10:24–25

Christian community is not optional — it is central to the life of faith. Holy Hour is a place where all believers are welcome to gather, share, and grow together regardless of background or denomination.

How to Get Involved

Join a Gathering

The best first step is simply to show up. Check the Schedule to find a time that works for you and come ready to pray and worship with fellow believers.

Share Your Testimony

One of the most powerful ways to encourage others is to share what God has done in your life. Testimonies of faith strengthen the whole community and glorify God. During our gatherings there is always time to share.

Pray for One Another

We maintain a community prayer list. If you have a prayer request or would like to pray for someone else, please bring it to any gathering or share it with a community leader.

Our Values

Unity in Christ

We are united by our shared faith in Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior. We welcome Christians of every background — Baptist, Methodist, Pentecostal, Anglican, non-denominational, and more — because our bond is Christ, not tradition.
