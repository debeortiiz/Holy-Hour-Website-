The Lord's Prayer

Matthew 6:9–13

Our Father, who art in heaven,
hallowed be thy name.
Thy kingdom come, thy will be done,
on earth as it is in heaven.
Give us this day our daily bread,
and forgive us our trespasses,
as we forgive those who trespass against us.
And lead us not into temptation,
but deliver us from evil.
For thine is the kingdom, and the power,
and the glory, forever. Amen.

Prayers of Praise & Thanksgiving

Psalm 100

Shout for joy to the Lord, all the earth.
Worship the Lord with gladness;
come before him with joyful songs.
Know that the Lord is God.
It is he who made us, and we are his;
we are his people, the sheep of his pasture.
Enter his gates with thanksgiving
and his courts with praise;
give thanks to him and praise his name.
For the Lord is good and his love endures forever;
his faithfulness continues through all generations.

Intercessory Prayer

A Prayer for Others

Lord Jesus, I lift up to you those who are sick, grieving, or in need. Bring your comfort and healing to them. I pray for leaders in my community and nation — grant them wisdom and a heart for justice. Strengthen those who are persecuted for their faith, and draw all people to the knowledge of your saving love. Amen.

Prayer of Confession & Repentance

Based on 1 John 1:9

Merciful God, I acknowledge my sins before you. I have fallen short of your glory in thought, word, and deed. Thank you that if I confess my sins, you are faithful and just and will forgive me and cleanse me from all unrighteousness. Restore me by your grace and renew a right spirit within me. Amen.

Prayer of Surrender & Trust

Proverbs 3:5–6

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight."

Lord, I surrender my plans and worries to you today. I choose to trust in your goodness and your perfect will for my life. Guide my steps and let me walk in the light of your Word. Amen.

Evening Prayer

A Prayer to Close the Day

Heavenly Father, as this day draws to a close, I thank you for your many blessings. Forgive the ways I have fallen short, and grant me rest and peace through the night. Watch over my loved ones and all who are in need. I place tomorrow in your hands. In Jesus' name, Amen.
